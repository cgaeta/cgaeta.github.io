Who am I?

I'm Chris Gaeta, a 25 year-old UX researcher, web developer, and web designer. I'm looking for somebody willing to pay for the skills I have carefully cultivated to identify and satisfy users' needs, build websites, and develop applications.

After a year and a half studying animation and game development at Purdue University, I turned my focus to web development and got my M.S. studying user experience research.

I've got an iOS app, Isee - Integrating Spatial Educational Experiences, on the iTunes store, and I was a front-end developer for DIA2 - Deep Insights Anytime, Anywhere. I taught a class in back-end development, too.

Why should you hire me?

On top of my programming skills and understanding of user research, I'm a clear communicator, a bilingual Spanish speaker, and an independent problem solver. I can handle pressure and stay calm in the face of difficult problems. I do my best to recognize my assumptions and consider problems from different perspectives.

I am a quick learner, and ready to ask for help if I am ever really struggling.

What am I passionate about?

I started taking dance classes when I was 19, and I've been teaching Salsa and Bachata classes for about four years now. It's a fun exercise and great way to meet people.
