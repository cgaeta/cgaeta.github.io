Deep Insights Anytime, Anywhere

DIA2 - Deep Insights Anytime, Anywhere - is a data visualization tool for researchers, students and NSF program officers. The DIA2 team has put the users at the center of our design process to make NSF's records as accessible as possible.

By the time I joined DIA2's technical team as a front-end developer, the design team had already developed three personas. These personas had emerged from affinity diagrams and are based on a collection of focus groups.

Based on these personas, I worked with the design team to create a new homepage for DIA2. We updated the homepage to advertise DIA2's functionality and the problems it will solve for our users.

After implementing our first design for the homepage, I worked with the design team to design to map out a task flow for users and design our login and registration forms for DIA2 users.
